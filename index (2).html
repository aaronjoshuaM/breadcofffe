Welcome to Coffee & Bakery

Serving the finest coffee, bread, and cookies daily.

Welcome to Coffee & Bakery, where tradition meets deliciousness in every bite! We are passionate about bringing the flavors of Filipino heritage into your daily life with our freshly baked breads, lovingly crafted from the finest ingredients. Our story began with a simple goal – to share the rich history of Filipino bread-making with the world. From the soft, sweet taste of pan de sal to the creamy, melt-in-your-mouth goodness of ensaymada, we are dedicated to perfecting these timeless recipes passed down through generations. Each loaf and pastry we make is a tribute to the vibrant culinary culture of the Philippines. At Coffee & Bakery, we believe that every bread should tell a story. Our bakers honor age-old Filipino baking techniques, combined with a touch of modern innovation, to create the perfect balance of tradition and taste. Whether you're craving a classic pan de coco or an indulgent hopia, we’ve got something for everyone to enjoy. Join us on this delicious journey, where every bite brings you closer to the heart of Filipino flavor!
